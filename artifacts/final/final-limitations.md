# Limitations

Governed .NET AIPW: **0.00157**; 95% CI: **-0.00377 to 0.00928**. Python grouped cross-fitted AIPW: **-0.01382**. The estimators use different nuisance-model fitting procedures, producing a material discrepancy. The primary AIPW estimate was a 0.157-percentage-point ATT risk difference. The 95% confidence interval ranged from -0.377 to 0.928 percentage points and included zero, so the analysis did not provide clear evidence of a nonzero causal effect under the stated observational assumptions. No claim is made that neighbor exposure causes adoption; observational identification assumptions, residual confounding, interference, overlap and measurement limitations remain.
