# Counterfactual simulation

Scenarios are `IncreaseNeighborExposure`, `AccelerateLargeMarketAdoption`, `IncreaseRegionalEarlyAdoption`, and `RemoveOneQuarterExposureDelay`. The intervention changes treatment only and preserves measured confounders. Results retain study/estimator/scenario lineage, baseline and intervention probabilities, estimated difference, uncertainty method, support, extrapolation distance, warnings, and limitations.

Support is `Supported`, `WeaklySupported`, `Extrapolative`, or `Unsupported`. These are model-based observational scenarios, not guaranteed policy outcomes.
