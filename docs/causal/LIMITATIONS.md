# Causal limitations

State diffusion creates interference. Medicaid utilization is not complete market access; approval is not commercial launch; suppression and missingness may be informative. Manufacturer, wholesaler, payer, supply, and policy processes are incompletely measured. DAG, adjustment, nuisance-model, treatment, and outcome specifications can be wrong. Positivity may be weak. Synthetic validation establishes software mechanics only.
# Cross-language validation limitation

C#/Python agreement can reveal implementation discrepancies but cannot validate an assumed DAG, rule out unmeasured confounding, restore positivity, or prove causal identification.
