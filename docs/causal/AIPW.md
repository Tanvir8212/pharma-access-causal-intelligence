# AIPW

AIPW combines the independently fitted propensity and binary outcome-regression nuisance models for explicit ATE or ATT risk-difference formulas. Outcome regression predicts potential-outcome probabilities under treatment and control; these are not observed counterfactual facts. Double robustness does not repair unmeasured confounding, interference, positivity failure, or incorrect timing.
# Cross-language formula check

Milestone 7 independently recomputes documented ATE and ATT row contributions from exported nuisance predictions. This verifies formula implementation, not identification assumptions.
