# Identification assumptions

Required acknowledgements are consistency, conditional exchangeability given the versioned adjustment set, positivity, temporal ordering, measurement validity, correct treatment/outcome definitions, and limited informative missingness. Strict no-interference/SUTVA is not assumed: peer diffusion is the spillover treatment itself, and a partial-interference approximation is used.

Manufacturer strategy, wholesaler behavior, payer policy, data suppression, and true commercial-launch timing may remain unmeasured confounders. No estimate is considered finalizable when positivity fails or assumptions are unacknowledged.
