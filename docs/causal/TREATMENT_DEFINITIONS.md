# Treatment definitions

Versioned treatments are `HighNeighborAdoptionExposure`, `HighRegionAdoptionExposure`, `HighSimilarStateAdoptionExposure`, and `EarlyLargeMarketExposure`. Each preserves continuous exposure plus binary threshold assignment, threshold, minimum peer count, lag, adjacency/similarity/market reference version, inclusion policy, reason, and warnings. Thresholds are declared without outcome-based selection and future adoption is prohibited.
