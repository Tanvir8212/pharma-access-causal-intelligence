# Adjustment sets

`peer-exposure-adjustment-v1` contains prespecified, leakage-safe pre-treatment variables: quarter since approval, launch cohort, state historical entry rate/volume/median delay/market weight/completeness, historical regional entry rate, previous-quarter national diffusion/volume/access gap/regional adoption, and baseline missingness/suppression indicators where available.

Every variable records availability, DAG role, inclusion reason, leakage status, missing policy, transformation, and version. Outcomes, future measures, post-treatment peer adoption, prediction probabilities, mediators, colliders, and treatment descendants are prohibited.
