# Research protocol

`ResearchProtocol` versions the question, population, observation window, predictive task/metrics, causal question/estimand/scale/estimators, inclusion/exclusion rules, state/entry/market-weight policies, feature/split/DAG/adjustment/treatment/outcome lineage, missingness, censoring, multiplicity, sensitivity, subgroups, and stopping rules. Lifecycle is `Draft → UnderReview → Approved`; rejection, supersession and archival are explicit. Approval requires an actor, UTC timestamp and reason and is never automatic.

The planned title is **From Approval to Access: Predictive and Causal Analysis of Generic Drug Diffusion and State-Level Medicaid Access Inequality**. FDA approval is a launch-reference proxy, not confirmed commercial launch. Medicaid utilization is a state-level access proxy, not complete national pharmacy availability.
