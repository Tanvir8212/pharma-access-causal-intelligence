# Mapping review

Statuses are `AutomaticallyResolved`, `ManuallyResolved`, `Ambiguous`, `Conflicting`, `Unmapped`, `Excluded`, and `Superseded`. Records preserve source hash/row, original and normalized identity, target, method, confidence category, evidence, reviewer/time, mapping version, and notes.

Only exact normalized matches may resolve automatically. Fuzzy candidates remain visible and require human review. Ingredient combinations preserve all components; strength/dosage form do not justify unsafe merging. RxNorm identifiers are never fabricated. Changed mappings require a new dataset and freeze version.
